Background

Octopus provides omnichannel solutions for the retail industry, and helping merchants bridge the gap between online and offline is one of the company's missions. During my full-time employment at Octopus Retail Management, I had the opportunity to work on multiple e-commerce projects, which gave me valuable experiences in this field.

My responsibilities were coming up with the proposed design and liaising with the customer's marketing team to finalize the document for design requirements.

The company has a website builder system that the user can use to update the content. I also had to discuss with the project manager and development team if the system met the customer's functional requirements and what new features we should build to complete the project.

Responsibility

Maintaining and improving the website builder system's user experience and the interface was one of my responsibilities. Besides, I often needed to create proposal designs for the sales team to pitch with prospective customers based on my knowledge of the current system.

A large e-commerce project often lasts from six months to more than a year. It required a lot of customized features of the website builder system. My role was to convert their requirements into functional and interaction flow with a straightforward interface.

CHALLENGES

Communication is essential to the success of the project. Therefore, we spend a lot of time discussing the functionality and workflow for each project. Sometimes, the last minus change from the customer could cause frustration to the development team.

Therefore, getting everything documented and avoiding changes at the last minus would help the team work more efficiently and help the product goes live smoothly.

These projects helped me learn more about the e-commerce industry while a merchant needs to handle thousands of SKUs and run different promotion and loyalty programs. Besides, I also gained experience designing the white-label system.
